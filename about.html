About Me

Hello! My name is Emily Lin, and I've developed a strong passion for software development. I graduated from UCLA in 2019 with a B.S. in Financial Actuarial Mathematics, a major heavy in mathematical theory. One of my favorite mathematics classes was Discrete Structures, and I then looked more into coding and computer science. I thus have been passionately studying coding and computer science fundamentals, including algorithms and data structures. I've worked in technical mathematics research aid, as an actuary at Unum, and I am currently a Capgemini Applications Consultant working on agile development projects. I am also passionate about beef noodle soup, rooftop or skyline views, and candles.

Overall, I've developed a strong passion for development, building my own projects. I would love to use my skills to help you!
